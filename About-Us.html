ABOUT VINSS MEDIA

We’re an up and coming special event Videography/photography and web design company, locally owned and operated.

While studying film at the Toronto Film School in Toronto, Canada, I often commuted using the subway. I would notice the facial expressions of those riding along with me and often wonder what they could be thinking. I would get the sudden urge to want to pull out my camera and start capturing what I saw.

I sought work as a photographer working with other companies while completing my studies. Soon my work was being shown in their studios and their portfolios. It was obvious that people thoroughly enjoyed my work. That was when it hit me that I should pursue my passion independently.

I have successfully done work for several weddings as both photographer and videographer. I have also designed several websites for local companies in the area. I have done work from Atlanta, Georgia to Monroe, Louisiana. From Toronto, Canada to Memphis, Tennessee to Jackson, Mississippi.

Let Vinss Media capture all your special life events with class and style. Let Vinss Media creative styles design and maintain your website.
